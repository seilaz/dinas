Banana Pudding

    Prep Time: 30 mins

    Additional Time: 3 hrs

    Total Time: 3 hrs 30 mins

    Servings: 12

    Yield: 1 (9x13-inch) dish

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups cold skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Â½ (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced

Directions

  1. step1 gather all ingredients
  2. step2Beat cream cheese in a large bowl until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, then pudding mix. Gradually mix in cold milk until smooth, followed by vanilla..
  3. step3Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
  4. step4Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish with vanilla wafers.
  5. step5Arrange sliced bananas evenly on top.
  6. step6Cover with pudding mixture.
  7. step7Top with remaining whipped topping.
  8. step8Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving
